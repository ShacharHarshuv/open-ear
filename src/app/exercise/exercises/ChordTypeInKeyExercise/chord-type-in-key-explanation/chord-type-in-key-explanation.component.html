In this exercise you will be required to identify the type of a chord being played (Like Major vs Minor).

Tip! Major chords tend to sound "brighter" and minor chords tend to sound "darker". But beware - the feeling of the chord also depends on context.

By default, all chords in this exercise will be from the same key (i.e. diatonic) - This is recommended as it simulates a more musical scenario where chords are not random. You can change that in the settings.
